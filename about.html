OUR LEGACY

Mr. Venkata Reddy Meenige:
A Visionary in Value Education

Mr. Venkata Reddy Meenige, Founder & Chairman of Rajarshi, brings over 35 years of transformative leadership.

Guided by Mr. Ratan Tata’s belief that "The greatest pleasure one can have is trying to do something that everybody says cannot be done”, he exemplifies hard work creating miracles for others.

An academician and passionate educationist, he has founded and guided institutions under Sri Balazi Educational Society, built on his unwavering belief in Value Education. He has also served on boards across banking, real estate, hospitality, and more. His visionary guidance and strategic acumen have been the inspiration behind every milestone we’ve achieved.



OUR JOURNEY

Over 35 Years of Educational Milestones

1991 – Navodaya High School

Founded to serve the “common man,” Navodaya’s results-driven culture sparked academic breakthroughs for generations. Navodaya High School has celebrated 30+ years of exemplary board results, with multiple batches achieving state-topping marks. Its alumni include executives heading top companies, award-winning researchers, and civil service leaders in India.

2003 – Life Line High School

Built on the belief that all-round education fosters balanced individuals. Here, students shine in academics, sports, arts, yoga, karate, and dance. Life Line High School boasts a 100% board-pass record since inception, an unheard of feat producing doctors, engineers, entrepreneurs, and artists who carry forward our ethos.

2018 – Oakdale Campus

A 21st-century learning ecosystem with:

  • A 21st-century learning ecosystem with Athletic track, courts for basketball, volleyball, tennis, badminton; cricket nets; football field; skating rink
  • A Spacious Campus with State-of-the-art labs, studios, and wellness spaces.

Across three generations of families, siblings and cousins have walked our corridors which is a testament to the trust parents place in our commitment to holistic growth.
