A Simple & Transparent Journey

We've designed a parent-friendly admission process in four clear steps.

1. Enquire & Explore

Fill out our Online Enquiry Form or call us. We invite you for a fast-track campus tour to meet our team and see our world-class facilities.

2. Assessment & Interaction

We conduct a friendly, 30-minute fun activity for kindergarten applicants. For other grades, a simple, age-appropriate assessment ensures the best fit for your child.

3. Confirmation & Fee Payment

Successful applicants will receive an Offer Letter within 48 hours. Secure your child's place with a token fee and choose a flexible payment plan that works for you.

4. Welcome to the Family

Join our Parent Orientation, get a campus walkthrough, and download our Parent App to stay connected and informed. Welcome to the Rajarshi legacy!

Age Criteria (as per NEP)

  • Nursery: 3+ years by March 31st
  • Pre-Primary I/II: 4+ / 5+ years
  • Grade 1: 6+ years
